Catering Services

At Nina's Menu, we're dedicated to making your next event a delicious and memorable one. We offer a variety of catering options to suit your needs, whether you're hosting a small gathering or a large party. Choose from our selection of Filipino dishes, including our signature lumpia, to create a customized menu that will leave your guests craving more.

Catering Options

  • Party Trays: Our party trays are perfect for events where guests can serve themselves. Choose from our selection of lumpia, pancit, adobo, and more.
  • Buffet Style: For larger events, our buffet-style catering includes a wider variety of dishes, allowing guests to sample and enjoy multiple options. This option includes setup, service, and cleanup.
  • Plated Meals: If you're looking for a more formal dining experience, our plated meal service includes individual portions of our delicious dishes, served by our professional staff.
